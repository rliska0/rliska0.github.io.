About Us

Welcome to little fox cafe! We are a local coffee shop located in Charlotte, NC. Stop by to cure your caffiene cravings, fill up on our fresh baked goods, or buy one of our products to brew your own little fox coffee at home! All of our products are only avaible in-store and we also sell mugs created by local ceramics artists. With indoor and outdoor seating, you are bound to get a perfect spot to nestle in and relax in!
